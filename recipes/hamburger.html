McDonald's Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun. Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

  • 1/2 teaspoon dried onion flakes
  • 2 teaspoons boiling water
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 dill pickle, slice (sour kind, not sweet)
  • 1/2 teaspoon prepared mustard (American yellow)
  • salt, to taste
